Trivandrum:

Trivandrum: Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of the Indian state of Kerala, is a vibrant cultural and political center.
Known for its rich history and architectural heritage, the city is home to several iconic landmarks, including the Napier Museum, Padmanabhaswamy Temple, and the scenic Kovalam Beach.
Thiruvananthapuram's lush greenery, pleasant climate, and educational institutions contribute to its reputation as a knowledge hub.
The city is also famous for its cuisine, with a wide array of delicious South Indian dishes.
Its mix of tradition and modernity makes Thiruvananthapuram a unique and enchanting destination for both tourists and residents.
