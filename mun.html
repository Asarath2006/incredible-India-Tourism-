Munnar:

Munnar: Munnar is a picturesque hill station in the Indian state of Kerala. Nestled in the Western Ghats, it's renowned for its lush tea plantations, misty mountains, and cool climate.
Munnar's breathtaking landscapes offer a serene escape, with attractions like Anamudi Peak, Eravikulam National Park, and the scenic Mattupetty Dam.
Visitors can explore tea factories, indulge in trekking, or simply unwind amidst the verdant scenery.
This tranquil destination is a haven for nature enthusiasts and a popular tourist spot, offering a blend of natural beauty and cultural experiences in the heart of southern India.
