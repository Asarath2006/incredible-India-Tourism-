ATHIRAPPILLY WATERFALLS:
Athirapally Waterfalls is a stunning natural wonder located in the Indian state of Kerala. Cascading down from a height of approximately 80 feet,
it is often referred to as the "Niagara of India." Surrounded by lush greenery and dense forests, the waterfall is a captivating sight, especially during the monsoon season when it's at its most powerful.
Athirapally is a popular tourist destination, offering opportunities for trekking, wildlife sightings, and relaxation in the lap of nature.
It has also been featured in several Indian films due to its breathtaking beauty, making it a must-visit for nature enthusiasts and travelers.
